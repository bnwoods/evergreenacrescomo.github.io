Purpose & Guidelines

Our Purpose

To promote and encourage the safety, improvement, communications, and general quality of life in this neighborhood. We are committed to be a liaison with government agencies on issues that affect our neighborhood.

What are our membership guidelines?

  • You must be a homeowner on Redwing Drive, Kelsey Drive, Lentz Drive, Irma Drive, or Shamrock Drive.
  • Payment of yearly dues in the amount of $40.00 per household. Please make payment out to “Evergreen Acres Association” and mail or deliver to the treasurer.
