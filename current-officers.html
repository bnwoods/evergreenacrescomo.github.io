Current Officers

Each officer has specific duties related to their position. For a more involved list of duties, please refer to the welcome packet.

President

The president’s duties include:

  • Make meeting agenda and conduct the meeting
  • Sign off on Compliance to the Covenants issues
  • Implement business issues voted on by the membership
  • Work with the various committees
  • Work with the Neighborhood Watch Program and the Block Captains
  • Make neighbors aware of the covenants
  • Keep copies of all non-compliance letters
  • Keep copies of all correspondence with other outside groups concerning the neighborhood

Vice President - Cedric Lakes

The vice president’s duties include:

  • Assist the president
  • Fill in for the president in his/her absence
  • Aid in committee planning
  • Assist new residents in getting access to the welcome packet

Treasurer - Pam Hiller

The treasurer's duties include:

  • Receive and deposit membership dues
  • Have access to bank account at Boone County National Bank
  • Pay bills including mowing of the right-of-way
  • Justify the monthly bank statements
  • Share quarterly financial reports with the membership
  • Notify all residents of the yearly membership dues in May/June

Secretary

The secretary's duties include:

  • Take and compile business meeting minutes
  • Be prepared for reading the minutes in the business sessions
  • Notify all residents of any upcoming meetings, picnics, work sessions, etc
  • Share minutes of meeting with all residents
  • Assist in keeping email addresses current
